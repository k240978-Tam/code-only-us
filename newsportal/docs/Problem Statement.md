# Problem Statement Document

## Problem Title
**Nepal Bulletin Board – Online News Portal**

---

## Problem Description
Nowadays, people are depending greatly upon various online portals to get the news about recent affairs and important updates. After the Genz Protest in Nepal, People are seeking the news and updates through online. There are many news websites and digital media outlets; however, many of them have been reported to suffer from the following major problems: outdated website design, overcrowded interface, poor mobile response rate, poor arrangement of contents, and many advertisements. Such difficulties hinder easy navigation through the website and decrease usability.

At the same time, there is an increasing demand for a centralized online platform which provide well-structured news according to different categories for different sets of audience. People expect to get local, national and even international news through the website without experiencing any sort of inconvenience. Such requirements are hardly fulfilled by existing solutions.

The problem is not only about the publishing news via an online portal, It is also about creating a platform where users can find the news trustworthy, organizes and user friendly. Otherwise, the users will experience misinformation, lack of timely information, poor usability and difficulty to find the relevent content to their interests.

---

## Target Users
The main users affected by this problem are:

- General reader, who want daily updates of nepal
- Students who need reliable news and current affairs information
- Professional workers, who require quick access to important headlines
- Nepali diaspora living abroad who want to stay connected with Nepal
- Journalists and administrators who need a platform to publish and manage news content

Secondary users may include:

- News agencies and freelance content contributors
- Businesses and advertisers targeting digital audiences
- Researchers looking for archived and categorized news information

---

## Current Solutions
Current solutions available in the market include:

- Traditional newspaper websites
- Social media news feeds
- Online news portals and mobile applications
- Video-based news updates via YouTube and Facebook

While these platforms provide access to information, many of them still suffer from:

- Poor user interface or UI? design
- Lack of personalization and modern web features
- Limited search and filtering functionality
- Navigation issues
- Overdependence on advertisements, reducing user satisfaction

---

## Competitors in the Market
The Nepalese online news industry is already competitive, with several established digital news portals and media organizations.

### Major Competitors
- **OnlineKhabar** – A highly popular digital news portal in Nepal with a lot of content categories
- **eKantipur** – One of Nepal’s leading online news platforms with strong brand recognition
- **Ratopati** – A frequently updated online portal covering national and international news
- **Nepal News** – One of the older online news platforms in Nepal

### Other Notable Competitors
- **The Kathmandu Post** – English-language digital news portal
- **Nepali Times** – News and feature-based online publication
- **Nepal Press** – Modern digital news website
- **ImageKhabar** – Popular for current affairs and breaking news

### Specialized / Emerging Competitors
- **Baahrakhari** – Fast-growing digital news platform
- **Arthik Abhiyan** – Focused on business and economic news
- **OSNepal** – News platform popular among global Nepali readers

---

## Competitor Limitations / Market Gap
Although these competitors are established in the market, several important gaps still exist:
- Overcrowded interface and too much advertisement
- Bad user experience on mobile devices
- Low level of interactive and engaging content
- Lack of proper content categorization and searching options
- Not having a modernized bulletin format
- No consistent accessibility to local information
  
These gaps create an opportunity for a more modern and user-focused online news portal.

---

## Problem Statement (Core Issue)

However, there is no proper existence of a **news portal that is up to date and structured in the modern sense in Nepal** where the readers can get access to various types of news, categorized into different sections.

---

## Proposed Solution
The **Nepal Bulletin Board – Online News Portal** is proposed as a web-based digital news platform designed to address the above problems by providing:

- News categorization like politics, business, sports, entertainment, technology, and local news
- Clean interface that is optimized for desktop and mobile use
- Search functionality that makes finding news faster and easier
- Up-to-date news displayed in bulletin style
- Admin panel for managing news creation, editing, and publishing
- Overall, the new platform would be more user friendly than current solutions

The platform aims to improve how users in Nepal and abroad consume digital news by making information more organized, accessible, and reliable.

---

## Why This Project is Important
It is significant due to the emergence of the need for **digital journalism and convenient information access** in Nepal. The increasing trend among users of changing from paper publications to digital publications indicates the necessity for an application that would provide **fast, user-friendly, reliable, and convenient access to news**.

The **Nepal Bulletin Board – Online News Portal** has the potential to serve as a modern digital news hub that not only informs readers but also improves the overall online news-reading experience.
